Solar Eclipse Traffic Disparities in Pennsylvania

The recent solar eclipse gave us rare but expected glimpse at unusual traffic disparities in PA. I used PennDOT's Traffic Information Repository (TIRe) to map and graph the hourly directional traffic volume from April 8th. Below is a map of the locations of traffic counters for this post. I picked a sampling of counters in a variety of locations on highways going roughly north and south.
